The AI Literacy
Heptagon.

Seven dimensions, four levels, one figure — a self-assessment instrument for LIS, rendered as an interactive publication plate.

Reading the plate

Seven axes, one per dimension. Four concentric rings: Unaware, Beginner, Intermediate, Expert. The terracotta band marks the Beginner baseline expected of every LIS member.

The profile

The red polygon is the team's current self-assessment. Where a vertex dips below the terracotta band, the dimension is below baseline — the marginalia on the plate calls it out.

Interaction (v1)

Hover any vertex for the Bloom-aligned anchor text. Click to jump to that dimension in the assessment. Export as JSON or a typeset PNG plate.
